Teaching

I have been involved in numerous courses on probability and statistics, analysis and linear algebra at at Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Applied Earth Sciences, TBM and Architecture. Some specific courses
- within the Mastermath programme the course applied statistics;
- within the minor finance at TU Delft the course on Financial time series;
- within the master Applied Mathematics at TU Delft the course Statistical Inference, which is a common core course. For the course taught in 2016 I used lecture notes together with the text "Essentials of Statistical Inference" by Young & Smith.
